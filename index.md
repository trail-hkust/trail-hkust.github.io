<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">Short Bio</h2>

Zixuan Yuan​ is currently an Assistant Professor at the FinTech Thrust, HKUST (Guangzhou). His research integrates advanced AI methodologies​ with critical financial challenges, focusing on developing scalable and impactful solutions for AI-driven wealth management, quantitative trading, and regulatory technology (RegTech). His primary research interests include **Financial Text Analytics**, **LLM Reasoning**, **Agentic Orchestration**, **Cost-effective Vertical RAG**, and **Model Interpretability**. Dr. Yuan has published 20+ papers in premier venues, including IEEE TKDE, IEEE IOTJ, ACM SIGKDD, SIGIR, WWW, AAAI, ACL, EMNLP and IJCAI. His work bridges theoretical innovation and practical deployment through industrial collaborations with leading tech/financial institutions, such as Baidu Research, IBM Research, RavenPack, HSBC, CITIC Group, and China Merchants Fund Management. Dr. Yuan has also co-founded Guangzhou Lingyue Technology, an AI-powered wealth management startup that translates cutting-edge AI research into commercial applications for institutional and individual investors.
